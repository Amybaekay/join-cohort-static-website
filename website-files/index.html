WHAT WOULD YOU LIKE TO KNOW MORE?

Frequently asked questions

What is Cohorts and who is it for?

Cohorts is a mentorship initiative designed to guide individuals through key stages of their tech and career journeys. It is for people who want more from their careers, those seeking clarity, direction, and a strong community to grow with.

How do I apply to join a cohort?

You can apply by choosing the cohort that best aligns with your current goals and reviewing the curriculum to ensure it's the right fit. Once your payment is confirmed, you'll be added to a private Slack channel where you'll connect with other students and begin your journey.

Is this program for women only?

No, this program is open to both genders.

Can I join more than one cohort at a time?

Yes, you can. Each cohort runs twice a year, so you’re free to join multiple cohorts. Many participants may find value in joining different tracks as their goals evolve within the year.

What is the duration of each cohort program?

Each cohort lasts for 4 weeks in total.

What topics are covered in each cohort pathway?

Topics are relative to a specific cohort, so please click on the cohort you’d like to join to learn more and view the detailed learning curriculum. This will help you understand the focus areas, skills covered, and how the program aligns with your personal and professional goals.

Which cohort is right for me based on my experience level?

This solely depends on you and your current goals. Take some time to reflect on where you are in your career and what skills or support you need most right now.

Do I need a technical background to join a cohort?

Cohorts like BigTech, NextGen, and the UK Global Talent Visa require a technical background due to the specialised skills and knowledge needed to achieve the program’s outcomes.

Is this program free or paid?

Cohorts is a paid program.

Are the sessions live or pre-recorded?

The sessions are live, and recordings can be accessed after the class.

Will I get access to mentors or 1:1 guidance?

Yes, once your membership is confirmed, you will be scheduled for a 1:1 session with Chisom. This personalized meeting will give you the opportunity to discuss your goals, ask questions, and receive tailored guidance to help you make the most of your Cohorts experience.

Are there networking opportunities with other members?

Absolutely! The community is intentionally designed to help you collaborate and build meaningful networks with others who share similar goals. Through group discussions, events, and the private Slack channel, you'll have plenty of opportunities to connect, share experiences, and support each other’s growth.

Can I stay involved in the community after the cohort?

Yes, we have a structured alumni program that keeps you connected long after your cohort ends.

What’s unique about Cohorts?

Cohorts is a one-of-a-kind learning platform. It stands out because it combines a structured curriculum with a supportive community and personalised mentorship, all tailored specifically to tech career pathways.

How is this different from other mentorship platforms?

Distinguished from other programs, it offers clear, goal-oriented tracks, regular 1:1 guidance, and ongoing alumni support, ensuring you’re not just learning but actively progressing with accountability and connection.
